- **Fix: PM2/Windows Unawaited Promise Early Termination** ŌĆö Diagnosed why transactional emails fail on live PM2+Windows while test emails and Zalo/Telegram work: all 6 notification trigger call sites (`POST /api/sales-requests`, `POST /api/orders`, `PATCH /api/orders/[id]/status`, `PATCH /api/sales-requests/[id]`, `POST /[id]/cod`, `POST /[id]/pay`) were firing notification promises without `await`, then immediately calling `return jsonResponse(...)`.
